The Best Movies According to Tushar

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shutter Island

I like Shutter Island because its mind-bending plot and psychological depth keep me on the edge of my seat, making me question reality long after the credits roll.

Inception

A mind-bending thriller by Christopher Nolan that explores the layers of dreams within dreams.

Fight Club

A psychological drama that delves into identity and the darker sides of the human psyche.
